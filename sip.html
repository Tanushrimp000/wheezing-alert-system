About the Project

The Wheezing Pre-Detection & Alert System is an AI-powered wearable healthcare solution designed to assist asthma and respiratory patients by providing early warnings before a wheezing attack occurs.

The system continuously monitors respiratory sounds, environmental conditions, and vital parameters using wearable sensors. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning algorithms analyze these patterns to predict wheezing episodes 30 to 60 minutes before they occur, allowing users to take preventive action.

Project Abstract

Traditional respiratory monitoring devices are reactive and provide alerts only after symptoms become noticeable. Our system introduces a proactive approach by continuously analyzing lung sounds and environmental factors to predict respiratory distress in advance.

Through AI-driven analysis, the wearable device classifies risk levels and instantly notifies users, caregivers, and healthcare professionals through mobile alerts, LEDs, and buzzers.

Problem Statement

  • Asthma patients often experience sudden wheezing episodes without warning.
  • Current monitoring systems are reactive rather than predictive.
  • Delayed detection can lead to emergency hospitalization.
  • Elderly and rural patients lack continuous respiratory monitoring.
  • Need for an affordable and wearable healthcare solution.

Key Features

  • Continuous Respiratory Sound Monitoring
  • AI/ML-Based Wheeze Prediction
  • 30–60 Minute Early Warning Alerts
  • Temperature, Humidity and Airflow Sensing
  • Mobile Application Notifications
  • LED and Buzzer Alert System
  • Affordable Wearable Chest Patch
  • Real-Time Risk Assessment

How the System Works

  1. Wearable sensors capture respiratory sounds.
  2. Signal processing removes unwanted noise.
  3. AI/ML models analyze breathing patterns.
  4. Risk level is classified as Low, Medium, or High.
  5. Alerts are generated and sent to users.
  6. Preventive action can be taken before severe symptoms occur.
